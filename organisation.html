Organization

The Center for Autism is aimed at the target group with neurodevelopmental disorders, ie. Autism spectrum disorders, ADHD, Tourette's syndrome and developmental disability. Some of our users also have comorbid problems such as anxiety, OCD and depression. We are an organization with a multidisciplinary staff with different tasks and different focus, but as a whole have a common academic framework. The joint professional platform commits the employees of the Center for Autism to work within this framework based on the individual user's both common and individual needs. Our focus is based on the individual person and a high professional standard.

Suggestion for Our Users

For users who use one of our offers on a daily basis and who, in addition to an autism spectrum disorder, also have a comorbid problem, we would recommend that the user be treated simultaneously by a psychiatrist. An untreated comorbid disturbance can weaken the user's benefit from our offerings.

Collaborators

We collaborate with case officers, teachers, educators, psychologists and psychiatrists who work with and around our target group in their daily lives.

Additional Information

We are approved as a fund company with a social, charitable purpose. Our possible financial profits go to raise the work of people with autism. Our Board of Directors has the overall responsibility for the organization. We are suppliers to the Ministry of Social Affairs' National Knowledge and Special Advisory Organization VISO.
